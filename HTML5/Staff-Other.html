Staff Members
Brian K., Service Technician

Brian has been a service technician with NH Exteriors since 2014. Born in the Lakes Region, the single dad with two young daughters grew up in the construction industry, working alongside his dad, doing HVAC work when the two of them lived in South Carolina.

During his time at NH Exteriors, Brian has developed a reputation for going the extra mile for customers, many calling his boss to thank him for Brian’s work ethic, can-do attitude and famous smile. In his spare time, when not with his girls, he likes to fish and bow hunt in the Vermont woods.

Lilly, Booking Agent

With five active daughters and three large dogs: two Dobermans weighing in at 105 and 84 lbs. and a Boxer mix topping the scales at 72 lbs., Lilly is no shrinking violet when it comes to meeting goals and getting things done.

A new member of NH Exteriors’ call center but definitely not new to the work, she is effective at her job, saying “we tend to like what we’re good at.” Really listening to customers is her priority, having gotten her feet wet at an education-based call center years ago. There she had the opportunity to connect prospective students with schools that would help them improve their lives, just as helping people improve the safety, quality and beauty of their homes can have a similar impact.

In her time away from work, she takes walks with her husband, seeks out garage sales and loves to sing contemporary gospel, having once sung in a band.

MaryEllen G., Booking Agent

MaryEllen G. joined the company in August, 2019, as a booking agent—perhaps with an advantage! As her husband is a general contractor, she has worked alongside him tearing down walls, installing bay and bow windows, doors, granite countertops, tiling floors and remodeling bathrooms. In short, she knows firsthand about home improvement.

She also loves the cohesiveness of the NH Exteriors call center team, saying people have daily goals to meet but if someone is struggling, everyone else jumps in to help. She defines the company as “very family-like where everyone wants to see you succeed.”

The mother of four “outdoorsy” girls ranging in age from 2 to 16 years old, nightly family walks with their Boxer are high on the agenda for the Hookset resident, as are cookouts and visits to swim in grandma’s pool and swing high on her swing set.

Brian C., Booking Agent

Brian C. knows a thing or two about education—both his own in mastering information about NH Exterior’s products and services, and in relaying it to the consumers he says he can’t wait to talk to every day. He says the training “and continuous phenomenal support” he gets as a booking agent is the best ever, ensuring his ability to build a good rapport on the phone.

The Kennebunk, Maine native moved to Manchester in 2004, wetting his feet in the lawn care industry where he began to learn about what’s most important to homeowners. “People are making an investment, and I need to make sure they are well informed and comfortable with everything we do. That’s what makes my job something I really look forward to.”

In his downtime, Brian and wife Lisa take their two boys and two girls to the family campsite and travel trailer in Ware where he likes to fish and grill burgers and steaks. When not at work or out camping, he can be found preparing baked salsa chicken with shredded cheese alongside his children, making time spent with them his priority.

Geoffrey D., Booking Agent

Asked to describe his chosen field, Concord native and booking agent Geoffrey D. considers his work a “helping profession,” but not necessarily in the traditional sense of the term. Coming to NH Exteriors in May with 15 years of installation, design/build and sales experience in his DNA, Geoff had worked for his family’s security alarm company since age 18, meeting the needs of thousands of customers. Desiring to take his skills and experience into a new realm, he says he finds the emphasis always on honesty, loyalty, product knowledge, transparency and, yes, helping people—just the way he was raised.

“My goal with NH Exteriors is to take all that as far as I can, for as many years as I can, growing and moving into sales where I hope I can be even more of an asset to our customers.”

The outdoor enthusiast who spends his down time hiking, kayaking, camping, playing golf, tennis, soccer and basketball says his training by Marketing Manager George Jobel and Assistant Marketing Manager Mark Raymond far exceeded anything he’d have imagined. “I was highly vetted in order to come to work here. They know what they’re doing. That kind of integrity translates to all of our customers.”

Larry W., Booking Agent

Larry W. uses the word “senior” in multiple applications: in conjunction with the Baby Boomer generation of which he’s a proud part, and to celebrate his booking agent longevity with NH Exteriors. Joining the team in 2011, the 40+ year NH native says he loves the company’s flexible hours so he can spend time playing Scrabble and Cribbage with his wife of 30 years and all their friends. “I feel truly supported here and have no plans to retire anytime soon. I also love a job like this where I can use my mind to dig deep and find the best answers to customers’ questions.”

Some years ago Larry was part of the Muscular Dystrophy Association’s (MDA’s) “Jail & Bail” team. In that role he empowered business owners and managers to raise funds to send afflicted children to summer camp to help change their lives. He considers the outreach he made into the community a solid training ground for his current job, where he gets to talk with as many people as he can each week about the home improvement solutions that can change their lives as well.

Married and an avid hiker, he and his 8- and 12-year-old routinely avail themselves of NH’s “52 With a View,” hiking the state’s peaks from 2500 to 4000 feet. Kenneth enjoys fly fishing, cooking and baking, including making carrot cake from scratch. He cheerfully describes his life outside of work as “making cookies and walking a long way.”
